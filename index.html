10 FAQ

Questions owners ask before signing up.

Will my customers actually opt in?
Yes — and more than you'd expect. There's no app to download and nothing to fill out beyond a name and phone number. Average opt-in rate at the tap card is over 60%. Restaurants typically collect 50–80 numbers in their first week without their staff saying a word.
Do my customers need to download an app?
No. Customers tap a card or scan a QR code — that's it. Nothing to install on their end. That's exactly why opt-in rates are so high: zero friction.
How long does setup take? I don't have time for this.
Under an hour for your first campaign. QR codes and the kiosk flow are live the moment you sign up. Your NFC tap cards ship within 3–5 business days. Once the cards are on the tables, the list builds itself.
Is this legal? Do I need permission to text people?
Yes — and yes. Every opt-in includes explicit TCPA-compliant consent language. Customers can always reply STOP to unsubscribe instantly. We handle all the compliance language and opt-out mechanics for you.
Does it integrate with my POS?
We integrate directly with Square, Toast, and Clover for automatic visit tracking. If you're on a different system, you can still use Boostly — you'll track revenue attribution manually through the dashboard, which takes about 2 minutes after a campaign.
What if I'm not good with technology?
We built Boostly for owners who run restaurants, not software companies. If you can send a text message, you can use Boostly. The campaign builder is three steps: write your message, pick your audience, hit send. And if you ever get stuck, a real person answers the phone.
