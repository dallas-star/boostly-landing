FAQ

Common questions.

Do my customers need to download an app?
No. Customers tap a card, scan a QR code, or enter their info on a kiosk. There's nothing to download on their end — which is why opt-in rates are so high.
How long does setup take?
Most restaurants are up and running within a day. Your tap cards ship within 3–5 business days. QR codes and kiosk flows are ready the moment you sign up.
Is this legal? Do I need customer permission?
Yes — and yes. Boostly is fully TCPA-compliant. Every opt-in includes explicit consent language, and customers can always reply STOP to unsubscribe. We handle all of that for you.
Does it integrate with my POS?
We currently integrate with Square, Toast, and Clover. More integrations are in the works. Even without a POS connection, you can track revenue attribution manually through the dashboard.
What if I have multiple locations?
Our Pro plan is built for multi-location operators and franchises. You get a single dashboard across all locations with the ability to run campaigns at the location level or across all of them at once. Reach out to our sales team for group pricing.
